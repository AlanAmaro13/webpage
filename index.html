About Me

I am a physicist and AI researcher focused on translating complex science into deployable, high-impact AI products. My expertise bridges full-stack technical execution with strategic product development, with a proven track record of building scalable systems for computational chemistry, materials science, and medical diagnostics.

My most significant achievements involve end-to-end ownership of scientific AI tools — from synthetic data generation and custom neural network architecture design to production-ready desktop applications and cloud-deployed agentic systems. I hold a Bachelor's in Physics from Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM), where I specialized in Artificial Intelligence, Materials Science, Data Science, and Computational Chemistry.

I am driven by the challenge of turning groundbreaking research into reliable, user-centric technology. Actively seeking collaborations with innovators and organizations that want to leverage cutting-edge AI to solve the most pressing challenges in science and industry.
